# Contributing

Thanks for helping expand this list! Here is how to submit a new distribution.

## What Qualifies

- ✅ Must be based **directly on Debian** (not Ubuntu or another derivative)
- ✅ Must maintain its own identity and tools
- ✅ Must have an active website or project page
- ❌ No Ubuntu-based distributions (that is a different family tree)

## How to Submit

1. **Fork this repository**
2. **Edit README.md** - Add your distro to the appropriate category
3. **Use this format:**
   ```markdown
   * **[Distro Name](https://official-website.com)** - Brief description of what makes it unique.

## What to Include

When submitting, please provide:

- **Official website** - Link to the project's main page
- **Category** - Which section does it belong in?
- **Description** - 1-2 sentences on what makes it worth a look
- **Your reasoning** - Why should this be included? (in the PR description)

## Categories

- Daily Drivers (Ease of Use)
- Modern & Atomic (Immutable)
- High Performance & Gaming
- Multimedia & Creative
- Minimalist
- Performance & Old Hardware (Lightweight)
- Security, Privacy, and Stealth
- IT and SysAdmin Toolbox
- Servers, Cloud, and Self-Hosting
- Network & Radio Engineering
- Embedded, IoT, and Media
- Mobile Distributions
- Philosophy and Geopolitics

## Questions?

Open an issue if you're unsure about a category or whether a distro qualifies.
